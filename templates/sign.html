Sign Files Using RSA Secret Key

This area is reserved to the digital signing of documents, using a secret key. To sign a Document, just:
  • Upload your files;
  • Select the resume algorithm for your signature;
  • Select your secret key;
  • Click on sign file;
Digital signatures are the best way to deploy a unique signature to your files, directly linking them only to the properties of your secret key. That means that only the ones who have your public key can check if you signed the document. A slightly change on the original document or signature, will produce a total different output.
